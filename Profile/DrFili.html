Bio

Dr. Ortiz-Chi is a Researcher in Mexico (Investigador por México) assigned to the Department of Applied Physics at Cinvestav Mérida, where he conducts research on the design of materials. He is a Level 2 member of Mexico’s National System of Researchers (SNII). During his previous assignment (DACB-UJAT), he led five research projects and supervised undergraduate (2) and master’s (4) theses. His scholarly output includes 39 articles with more than 800 citations.

Research Lines

  • Computational materials design.
  • Surface and interface physicochemistry.
  • 1D and 3D materials for energy applications.

Relevant Projects

  • Search for new material structures.
  • Studies of nanomaterials for energy applications.
  • Search for rotational isomers in molecules with torsional freedom.
  • Search for mixed clusters of titania, alumina, and zirconia.
  • Global optimization for the prediction of clusters and molecules.
